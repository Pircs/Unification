Weapon Skills Window

1. Operation:

(1) Click on X to exit weapon Skills window.
(2) Click on the icon of a weapon to view its name, level and experience percentage to next level.
(3) There are three icons on the bottom: One-handed, Two-handed, and other, displaying all the weapon skills you have mastered under those three categories.
(4) If the weapon skills are listed more than one page, click up or down arrows to view more weapon Skills.


2. Display:

(1) An icon of the weapon is displayed on the left of the window.
(2) The level of a weapon skill is shown left to its icon in the form of golden stars.
(3) The percentage shown in the window = current experience points gained/all the experience points needed to upgrade to the next level.
