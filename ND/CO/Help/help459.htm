About Refinery

Refinery provides extra attributes and buffs.
The powers gained from a Refinery item is temporary, and only lasts for 7 days before the magic disappears. To make such a TemporaryRefineryState permanent, you should find the Refinery Stabilizer in the Market (299,212). He'll channel the power of Permanent Stones and make the Refinery permanent.
