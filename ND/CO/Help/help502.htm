Garment Shop

1. Select a Garment and click [Buy] to purchase the Garment at the original price!
2. Click [Try On] and select a Garment, you will be able to try a Garment on for 30 seconds.
3. You can drag your unwanted Garments and drop them in the Garment Trade In box to offset the price of the new Garment you want to buy! Click [Trade] to trade your old garment in for the new one! If the price of the old garment exceed that of the one you want to buy. The exceeded part will not be refunded.
