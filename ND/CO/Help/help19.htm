Options Window

Music: Turn down the music by pulling the slide toward the left, Turn up toward the right.

Sound: Turn down the sound effect by pulling the slide toward the left, Turn up toward the right.

800*600: The default resolution of the game is 800*600. Click on Window or Full Scr. to switch between window mode and full screen mode.

1024*768: When the resolution is changed from 800*600 to 1024*768, the game should be restarted. Then you can click Window or Full Scr. To switch between window mode and full screen mode under 1024*768 resolution.

Show name
Tick to show the names of all players, Cross not to show the names.

Shadow:
Full: You can see a clear and complete shadow of all the characters, NPCs and monsters on the ground.
Simple: You can only see a circle shadow of all the characters, NPCs and monsters on the ground.
Off: You cannot see any shadow of all the characters, NPCs and monsters on the ground.

Exit: Click on Exit to quit the game and back to the login screen.

Delete: Click on Delete to delete the character. Be aware of that all the data of the deleted character will be deleted, too.

Close: Click on Close to exit the Setup window.
