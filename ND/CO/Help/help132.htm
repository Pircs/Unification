Status Window

Quit this window: Click on X to exit the interface.
Basics: Click on Basics you can see your strength, agility, vitality and spirit.
Details: Click on Details to view your attack, defense, magic attack and magic defense.
Battle Power : Click on Battle Power to view your battle power evaluation calculated on the basis of the character level, rebirth times, and equipment etc.
PK Expl.: Your PK record.
Change: Change your title. Not available so far.

Basics:
Name: It is the name of the character you gave when you first created the character. Character's name should not be offensive, or it will be deleted. The character name cannot be changed.
Level: Your character level. 
Job: It is your job. 
PK Point: It is your current PK point. If you kill other players, your PK point will increase. But it will decrease as time goes by. 
Spouse: If you get married, it shows your spouse's name, or it shows None. 
Guild: The name of your guild.
Position: Your position in your guild.
Title: No available so far.
 
Basics: 
Strength: It determines your physical attack damage and the kind of weapons you are able to use. In another word, there is a minimum strength requirement for you to use a weapon.
Agility: It determines the accuracy of your physical attack. It is another requirement for you to use a weapon.
Vitality: It determines your hit points. The more vitality, the more hit points.
Spirit: It determines your magic ability. The more spirit, the better magic ability.

Details:
Attack: It refers to your physical attack.
Defense: It refers to your physical defense.
M-attack: It refers to your magic attack and it determines your magic damage.
M-defense: It refers to your magic defense and it determines your resistance to physical damage.
Dodge: It determines the chance to dodge a melee hit.
Agility: It determins your hit rate.
