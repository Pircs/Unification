Equipment Window

(1) Operation:

* Click on X to exit the interface.
* Double click on the equipped items to un-equip them.
* Stop the mouse on the equipped items to view the stats of the items.



(2) Display:

The 7 slots in the order of up-down and left-right are respectively named as: 1st slot, 2nd slot...7th slot.

1st slot:
It shows cap and earring.

2nd slot:
It shows necklace and bag.

3rd slot:
It shows ring, bracelet and heavy ring.

4th slot:
It shows right-handed weapon.

5th slot:
It shows clothes.

6th slot:
It shows left-handed weapon and shield. If you have equipped two-handed weapon on right hand, you can't equip any weapons on left hand.

7th slot:
It shows boots.
