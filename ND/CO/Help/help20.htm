Game Interface

Item:
Click on Item to view your carried items.

Options:
Click on Options, you can adjust music and sound effect, change display mode (window or full screen), shadow mode, delete character, set hotkeys, place and delete house furniture.

Action:
Click on Action and click on an action from action board, the character will show corresponding action.

Trade:
Click on Trade, you can do a deal with other players. Please refer to "Trade" for details.

Team:
Click on Team, you can establish or join a team. Please refer to "Team" for details.

Guild:
Click on Guild, you can get the information of your guild, join and leave a guild, expel members, donate money to your guild

Skill:
Click on Skill, you can get the information of the skills you have mastered.
