Trade Window

1. Operation:

Exit this Window
Click on X or Cancel to close the trade window.

Confirm amount
Type in the amount of money in the box under My Money, then click on Confirm. Be aware that once you confirm the amount, you are unable to change it. The only way to change the amount is to cancel the current trade and resume.

Agree:
Drag your item to the trade window, check if the item or money is right, then click on Agree.
If the other party clicks on Agree too, then the trade is completed.



2 Display:

Name of the other party:
Where it shows the name of the target trading with you.
The upper six slots display the items that the other party wants to trade with you. Place cursor on an item, you will see its details.

Money of the other party:
Where it shows the total amount of money the other party offered.
You can put items you want to trade in the slots underside, Remember once it is placed, it cannot be taken back unless you click close to cancel this trade.
