Sub-Class

After level 70, go to the JobCenter and choose one of the various sub-classes to acquire different bonus effects. There are six types of sub-classes; the Apothecary, the Chi Master, the Martial Artist, the Warlock, the Sage and the Performer. Each of them comes with a different bonus effect.

Performer

Effect: A command of unique Dances.

When to join: Character level above 70.

How to get promoted:

Performers have 9 Phases. After meeting the requirements for a certain phase, speak with the Lead Performer in the JobCenter to get promoted to a higher phase and gain better sub-class abilities.
