World CTF Contest

The top 3 winner guilds from each server are qualified to sign up at 20:30 on Tuesday, for the World CTF Contest.

The rules are the same as for Capture the Flag.

On the world server, players are unable to trade or purchase items. They are allowed to use only the items in their inventory to enter the server. During the World CTF Contest, lots of medicine packs will randomly appear on the map, which will disappear when players leave the server.

After the contest is ended, you can check Guild interface to learn the rankings.

 

Rules for Capture the Flag:

When the competition begins, all guilds will need to hurry and occupy a flag base. The guild that inflicts the most damage to the flag base will occupy that flag base. At the same time, the flag base you occupied will be marked in yellow on the mini map. During the competition, you can find flags in the arena. Seize a flag, then carry it back and plant it in the flag base your guild occupies to gain points for your guild. The Top 8 guilds will receive a grand prize! (PS: When the base has the `X2` status, the occupying guild will gain double points.)

The Flag Carrier can jump or ride a horse to return to the flag base faster. Flag Carrier quits the Capture the Flag Arena, disconnects, or is killed by the opposing guilds, he will drop the flag. So, the flag holder needs to be taken good care of!

You can gain Exploits through the flowing ways:

   #Kill the opposing guild member to gain 1~3 Exploits and Points (depends on the opponent`s BP).

   #Pick up 1 Flag to gain 3 Points and 3 Exploits. Carry a Flag to the base to gain 15 Points and 15 Exploits.

   #Revive your guild member to gain 2 Exploits.

   #Cast Stigma, Star of Accuracy, Magic Shield, Azure Shield on your guild members or remove their Soul Shackle state to gain 1 Exploit.

   #Use Soul Shackle on your opposing guild member to gain 1 Exploit.

 

PS: You can check the contest progress in the Guild interface.
