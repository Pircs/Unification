Guild Recruitment

View Recruitment Advertisement

1. Players without a guild can click `Group` in the main interface and select `Recruit` to enter the Guild Recruitment interface. You can view the recruitment advertisements released by different guilds, the details of the guilds and their requirements to join from there!
2. If the guild leader has activated the auto-recruit function, you will be accepted into the guild immediately after you apply to join, if you meet the recruitment requirements of the guild. If the auto-recruit function is not activated, you'll need to contact the guild leader and ask him/her to accept you into the guild!

Issue Recruitment Advertisement

1. If you are a guild leader, you can click `Group` in the main interface and select `Recruit` to enter the Guild Recruitment interface, then click `Recruit` to release a Recruitment Advertisement. Or you can enter your Guild Interface and click `Recruit` to release a Recruitment Advertisement.
2. You can enter your Recruitment Advertisement or slogan from the Guild Recruitment interface. This advertisement will help other players learn about the power of your guild. Be sure to make it attractive!
3. You can also activate the auto-recruit function, so that new members will be immediately accepted into the guild after they apply, if they meet your requirements. If this function is not activated, you will need to accept each member manually.
4. 500,000 silver will be charged to release a Recruitment Advertisement. The Advertisement will last for 7 days. If you change your advertisement before the advertisement is expired, the advertisement expiry time will be reset and you will be charged again. New Ad-rate = Original Ad-rate / 7 x Remaining Ad time. Your advertisement will be moved to the front if you can offer a higher Ad-rate than the other players. If 2 Ad-rate offers are the same, the advertisement released earlier will be placed at the front.
5. Click `Release` to issue your Recruitment Advertisement.
