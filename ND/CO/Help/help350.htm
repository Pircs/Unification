Profession

Fire Taoist:

Fire Taoist is proficient in combat skills, perhaps they are affected by magic, they are always in hot temper and cannot tolerate any evil behaviors. They declare they uphold justice as well as protect the purity of the world, but they often kill the others without any reason. People said that they bring the world not purity but destruction.

Fire Taoist can be promoted as Fire Wizard, Fire Master and Fire Saint.

Water Taoist:

Unlike other Taoist, they are always in calm temper and stand aloof from the worldly affairs. They never regard others as enemies. They are proficient in resurrection and aid magic. They have no powerful attack, so you cannot see them while you are fighting hard, but they will heal you when you are hurt.

Water Taoist can be promoted as Water Wizard, Water Master and Water Saint.
