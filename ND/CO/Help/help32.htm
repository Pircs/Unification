Message Board

All the players can see the messages in the message board. Please choose the related channels to submit your messages so that other players can find your message easily and quickly (Trade, Friend, Team and Guild).



(1) Message Board

Trade:
Click on this button to submit the trading messages

Friend:
Click on this button to submit the messages of making friends

Team:
Click on this button to submit the teaming messages

Guild:
Click on this button to submit the guild messages. Only the members in your guild can see the messages.

Others:
Click on this button to send other messages



(2) Submit messages

Click on Submit to toggle the message box on. Type in the messages and click on OK to submit the messages.

Only one can be active at a channel. Submitting another will cancel out the first one.


(3) View messages

Select one message and click on View to view the details. Click on Back to return to the bulletin.



(4) Delete messages

Click on Delete to delete the messages you submitted.
