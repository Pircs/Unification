Guild Reward

The reward set by the Guild Leader is shown for the guild members on the Guild Contest/Guild PK Tournament page. These rewards are shown in the descending order of the CP amount. If the CP amount is the same, the rewards will be shown in the descending order of the silver amount.

All guild members can click the Reward button on the page to check your guild reward, but only the Guild Leader can set or add rewards.
