Chat Window

(1) Chat channels:

Click a channel box to turn this channel on/off. If you turn off a channel, you won't receive any messages from this channel.

Talk:
Click on this box to turn on/off normal chat.

Guild:
Click on this box to turn on/off guild chat.

Team:
Click on this box to turn on/off team chat.

Friend:
Click on this box to turn on/off friend chat.

Whisper:
Click on this box to turn on/off whisper chat.

Filter:
Turn on Filter, you can receive your friend's private messages while the private message from other players will be filtered.

Action:
Click on this box to turn on/off action.



(2) Other buttons:


Chat History:
Click on Chat History to view all the chat messages.

Msg Filter:
Click on Msg Filter and then type in the players' names you want to filter.

Chat Log:
Click this button to view your chat history.



(3) Text Color:

Click on color box to change the text color.
