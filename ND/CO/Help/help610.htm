Champion's Arena

Champion's Arena is open! It's a brand-new and fair platform, where you don't need to care so much about your equipment, nobility rank or other things. It requires you to be focus on your skills! You will win Champion Points no matter you win or lose a match, which can be used to buy items in Arena Store! Don't hesitate to join!

Requirement: Level>=70

Mode: One-to-one

Features: There're 7 grades. You must starts from Grade 1. Champion Points are given no matter you win or lose. There're also extra points for winning streak. When your points are high enough, your grade goes up. The higher your grade is, the more points you will gain from each match.
