2. Where are the gems buried?

* Plain Mine: Phoenix Gem; Dragon Gem; Fury Gem.
* Forest Mine: Kylin Gem; Rainbow Gem.
* Canyon Mine: Violet Gem; Moon Gem.
* Desert Mine: Violet Gem; Moon Gem

(2) Remove a gem

* Drag and drop your item into the big slot.
* Click on the target gem you want to remove.
* Click on Remove to remove the gem. Be aware of that once you have it removed, it will disappear.
