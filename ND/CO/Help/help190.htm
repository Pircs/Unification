Chat Window

(1) Chat on/off

Click on Chat On/Off to open or close the chatting box and related buttons. Or you can press Enter key to open or close the chat field.

(2) Chatting Window:

Chat Target:
Click on up arrow button, you will see the saved names. Select the name for the target player, his/her name will be displayed on the chat target box.

There are four methods to target a player you wish to talk to:

* Type in the box the name of the player you wish to talk to.
* Open chat target list and select a target.
* Press Shift on the keyboard while click on the player.
* Click on "O" icon in the middle of the chatting box and click on a target to add him/her to chatting target list.

Add/Delete: There are two functions:

When the name of the player you wish to talk to is not in the list, adding button is "+", click on it, the name will be added to the list.

When the name is already in the list, deleting button is "-", click on it, the name will be deleted after you click on OK on the confirm window popped up.

Chat Box:
Type the message in the box.

Send:
Click on the down arrow button or press enter, the message will be sent out.

Clear:
Click on Clear, the message will be cleared.
