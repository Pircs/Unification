Guild Interface

You can check the Guild Level, Members, Fund and other information on the Guild Interface.

The Guild Donation includes the Silver Donation, PK Donation, Guide Donation, CP Donation and Arsenal Donation (The total Donation of the members’ inscribed equipment).

The available Donation contains the Silver Donation and the CP Donation.

Silver Donation: It is obtained by donating money.

PK Donation: It is obtained by killing members of enemy guilds.

CP Donation: It is obtained by donating CPs.

Guide Donation: It is obtained by helping recruits to level up.

Arsenal Donation: It is obtained by inscribing equipment.

The Rose Donation, Lily Donation, Orchid Donation and Tulip Donation are obtained by receiving flowers.
