Select a Job

1. Basic introduction:

(1) There are four jobs for you to Choose: Trojan, Warrior, Archer and Taoist.
(2) Click on the character of a job to view the introduction in the box below.
(3) The left characters are male, the right characters are female.
(4) Select a job and click OK button to select large or small char.

2. Job description:

Trojans: high HP, powerful attack, equip two-handed weapons.

Warriors: High defense, equip shield and possess a powerful skill named Superman that can increase attack ten times.

Archers: Equip bows and arrows, fly to dodge physical attack, a wide choice of powerful skills.

Taoists: can select to be Water Taoist or Fire Taoist at level 40. The former is easy to find groups for his/her auxiliary position and the latter possesses powerful attack.
