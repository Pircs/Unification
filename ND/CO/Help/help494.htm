Capture Flag

The Guild Leader can create a reward pool of silver and CPs for the Capture Flag before the game is started! The Guild Leader can increase the silver and CPs in the reward pool but not reduce it!
Guild members can gain exploits during Capture Flag. After the Capture Flag, guild members can get the reward from the reward pool according to the exploits they gained during Capture Flag.
You can check the exploits in the reward pool.
