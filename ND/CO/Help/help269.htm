Friend Window

(1) Operation

Click on X to exit the window.

Add Friends:
Click on "Make Friends" and click on the player you want to make friends with. His/Her name will be in your friend list after he/she agrees.

Up Arrow:
If your friend list is more than one page, you may click on this to page up your friend list.

Down Arrow:
If your friend list is more than one page, you may click on this to page down your friend list.

Delete:
Click on the name you want to delete and click on Delete, you will delete the friend once you click on OK.
If the name you click is the same as on the right side. The Status window on the right will be closed.

Enemy list:
Click on Friend List to shift to Enemy List.

Friend list:
Click on Enemy List to shift to Friend List.

Add enemy:
System will record the latest five players who killed you.

Delete enemy:
Select an enemy and click on Delete. The enemy will disappear from your enemy list. 



(2) Display:



Friend List:
If your friend is on-line, the color of his/her name is white. If your friend is off-line, the color of his/her name is red.


Friend's Status:

Head portrait:
If your friend is on-line, his/her head portrait will be shown on the top box of the status window. The first line is your friend's character name. The second line is the name of his/her spouse.

Level:
Your friend's current level.

Job:
Your friend's current job.

PK Point:
Display the current PK points of your friend.

Guild:
If your friend has joined a guild, his/her guild name will be shown here.

Position:
If your friend has joined a guild, his/her position will be shown here.




Enemy List:
If your enemy is on-line, the color of his/her name is white. If your enemy is off-line, the color of his/her name is red.


Enemy's Status:

Head Portrait:
If your enemy is on-line, his/her head portrait will be shown on the top box of the status window. The first line is your enemy's character name. The second line is the name of his/her spouse.

Level:
Your enemy's character level.

Job:
Your enemy's current job.

PK Point:
Display the current PK points of your enemy.

Guild:
If your enemy has joined a guild, his/her guild name will be shown here.

Position:
If your enemy has joined a guild, his/her position will be shown here.
