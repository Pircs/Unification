Team Window

(1) Create a team:

Create:
Click on Create to create a team, you will become a captain. A gold star will rotate above your head. You can dismiss your team, invite others to join your team or kick a member.

Dismiss:
Click on Dismiss to dismiss your team. If you are not a captain, the button won't appear on team window.

Invite:
Click "Invite" and click on the target player to send out your request. If your member list is not full, the target player will join your team after he/she agrees. If you are not a captain, Invite button won't appear on team window. Your request won't be sent out if the target player is already in a team.

Options:
Click "Options" to set whether other players can join your team, and your teammates can pick up your money or item.

Kick:
Click on the head portrait of the target member and click on Kick to kick him/her. If you are not a captain,Kick button won't appear on team window.



(2) Join a team:

Join:
Click on Join and click on the target captain. After he/she clicks on OK to agree, you will join his/her team provided that his/her member list is not full. If you are already in a team, Join button won't appear on team window. Your request won't be sent out if the target player is not a captain.

Leave:
Click on Leave to leave the team.

Follow
Click this button to follow the team captain.



(3) Setup:

Forbid:
Click on Forbid to forbid other players to join your team.

Money:
Click on this box to decide whether the team members can pick up your money.

Item:
Click on this box to decide whether the team members can pick up your items.
