Team PK Tournament

Four Groups: Over 130, 120~129, 100~119, Under 100.

Time: Every Saturday. Sign up from 20:25 until the Tournament starts.

The match start from the Over 130, and one by one until the Under 100, with an interval of 2 minutes.

No one can enter the field when the Tournament starts.

Each match will give the participants 1 EXP Ball`s EXP and 1,000 Honor Points.

If you dismiss a team, you forfeit your chance to compete.

New teams created in the field cannot participate in the Tournament.

In the Knockout, each match lasts for 3 minutes. If no one is dead when the match ends, the one with a higher score wins.

The next round starts when the previous rounds all end.

When there is less than 8 teams left in the field, the Final starts. The match in the Final lasts for 5 minutes. The Top 3 teams and the rest Top 8 winners will be handsomely rewarded. Only the leaders can claim your prizes, and make it before the start of the next week's PK Tournament or you will lose the prize.
