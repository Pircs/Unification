Detailed Stats

 (3) Details:

Attack:
It refers to your physical attack.

Defense:
It refers to your physical defense.

M-attack:
It refers to your magic attack and it determines your magic damage.

M-defense:
It refers to your magic defense and it determines your resistance to physical damage.

Dodge:
You can dodge the physical attack of the enemy if your dodge point is high. 

Agility:
It determines the accuracy of your physical attack.
