Create New Account

(1) Method 1:
Access the Conquer official site: http://co.91.com
Click on Account, then New Account, follow the instructions to fill in the required info.

(2) Method 2:
* Connect to the Internet. 
* Double click on Conquer play icon
* Click on Enter to access the login server interface
* Click on Register Acc. on the login server interface, you will be connected to the New Account page.
* Follow the instructions to fill out the required fields and click on Submit.
