You can sign up between 11:00-14:00 and 19:00-23:00, everyday!
You are going to team up with your friends to participate in the Qualifier, and share the points in your team. Daily Honor Points are awarded, according to your ranking.
Only team leaders are able to register for the tournament. Each team member will receive an invitation. The match starts after all members have responded. The team will forfeit if all members choose to quit.
Each match is held between two teams.
Each player can only enlist in one type of Qualifier at a time.
