Character Stats

(1) Basics:


Name:
It is the name of the character you gave when you first created the character. Character's name should not be offensive, or it will be deleted. The character name cannot be changed.

Level:
Your character level. 

Job:
It is your job. 

PK Point:
It is your current PK point. If you kill other players, your PK point will increase. But it will decrease as time goes by. 

Spouse:
If you get married, it shows your spouse's name, or it shows None. 



Basics: 

Strength:
It determines your physical attack damage and the kind of weapons you are able to use. In another word, there is a minimum strength requirement for you to use a weapon.

Agility:
It determines the accuracy of your physical attack. It is another requirement for you to use a weapon.

Vitality:
It determines your hit points. The more vitality, the more hit points.

Spirit:
It determines your magic ability. The more spirit, the better magic ability.
