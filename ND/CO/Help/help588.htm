Chi System

Level 120+ 2nd Reborn players are able to begin their study of the Chi System. At 00:00 everyday, players will receive 500 Chi Points (up to a maximum of 4,000).

Studying Chi helps you unlock the four Chi Gates: the Dragon Chi, the Tiger Chi, the Phoenix Chi and the Turtle Chi. Only one Chi can be unlocked, each time. When the Chi Value reaches a certain amount, you can open the next Chi.

After you unlock a Vital Chi, 4 kinds of attributes with a random type and effect will be attached to that Chi. It costs 50 Chi Points to refresh the attributes, once. The locked attributes will not be replaced while refreshing. The Chi Score is calculated and ranked according to the attribute's type and effect. The top players will obtain bonus attributes. Besides, the Chi Attribute will display different colors to show different grades of quality.
