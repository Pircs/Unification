Equipment Window

(1) Operation:

* Click on X to exit the interface.
* To un-equip the item, double click on the equipped item or drag the equipped item to the inventory.
* Stop the mouse on the equipped item to view the information of the item.


(2) Display:

To view the equipped items, click status button, then the second round button counted from the top left.

There are four small slots to show the equipped helmet, boots, necklace and ring.

Helmet
Increase defense.

Boots
Increase dodge.

Necklace
Increase defense.

Ring
Increases attack and agility.

There are three big slots to show the equipped armor, left-handed and right-handed weapon.

You can equip weapons in the right hand. Two-handed weapons and shields are equipped in the left hand. You cannot equip other weapons in the left hand if you have equipped two-handed weapons. The armor slot can be equipped with different armors. Your avatar varies with your armors.
