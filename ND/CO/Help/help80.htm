Guild

(1) Operation

Close:
Click on X to exit the interface.

Status:
Click on Status to view the basic status of your guild.

Donate:
Click on Donate, input the amount you want to donate and click on OK.

Bulletin:
Click on Bulletin to view the latest guild bulletins.

Enemies:
Click on Enemies to view all hostile guilds.

Allies:
Click on Allies to view all allied guilds.

Join:
Click on Join, then the guild leader you wish to join, you can join the guild after the guild leader agrees.

Leave:
Click on Leave to leave your guild.

Expel:
Click on Expel, input the member's name you want to expel and click on OK to expel this member.




(2) Display:

* Only Join button is active before you join a guild. After you become a guild member, Join button will be turned into leave button and you can click on Status, Bulletin, Donate, Allies and Enemies to view the relevant information, too. 

* Expel button won't display until you become a guild leader or deputy leader.

Position:
Display your current position in your guild. There are three positions: guild leader, deputy guild leader and guild member.

Donation:
Display your donated silver.

Guild Name:
It is your guild name.

Guild Leader:
It is your guild leader's name.

Guild Fund:
It is the guild fund.

Members:
It is the number of the guild members.
