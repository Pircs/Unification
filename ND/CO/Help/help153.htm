Inventory Window

1. Operation
Click on Item button to open your inventory and click on X to exit it.

Drop an item:
Click on an item and drag it outside the inventory, then release the mouse to drop it.

Equip an item:
Right click on an item you wish to use or drag it to the right box for the equipment slot.

View an item:
Stop your cursor on an item, then you can see detailed information of it.

Drop money:
Click on the icon of money and input the amount you want to drop in the window popped up, click on OK at last.


2. Display:

Amount of Money
The number on the left bottom corner of the window is the amount of your money.

Color of item name
If the name of an item is gold, it is a super item.
If the name of an item is red, it is an elite item.
If the name of an item is green, it is a unique item.
If the name of an item is blue, it is a refined item.
If the name of an item is white, it is a normal item.
