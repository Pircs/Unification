Skill Window

(1) Operation:

1. Click on X to exit this window.
2. Click on a skill. You can view its name, level, experience to next level.
3. There are three buttons on the bottom of the window: Kungfu, Magic and Skill. Click on the buttons to view all the spells under those categories.
4.If the skills learned are more than one page, click on up and down arrow to view more skills.


(2) Display:

1. Icon in a skill box represents the corresponding skills.
2. Icon in a level box shows the levels of the skill.
