Martial Arsenal

Each guild can only unlock an Arsenal daily.

After an Arsenal is unlocked, all the guild members can inscribe their equipment in this Arsenal. The sum of the donation of all the inscribed equipment is the Arsenal Donation. If the Arsenal Donation reaches 2 million, this Arsenal will obtain 1 Battle Power. When it reaches 4 million, this Arsenal will obtain 2 Battle Power. After it reaches 10 million, this Arsenal will obtain 3 Battle Power.

PS: The sum of the top 5 Arsenal`s Battle Power is considered to be the final Battle Power of the guild.

Each guild has 8 Arsenals: Headgear Arsenal (Headgears), Armor Arsenal (Armors), Weapon Arsenal (Weapons), Ring Arsenal (Rings, Heavy Rings and Bracelets), Boots Arsenal (Boots), Necklace Arsenal (Necklaces), Fan Arsenal (Heaven Fans) and Tower Arsenal (Star Towers). You should inscribe only Elite or above equipment to obtain the Donation, which is different according to quality, sockets and composition of the equipment.

Also, the guild level depends on the number of Overlord Arsenals. After donations reach 5 million, the Arsenal will become an Overlord Arsenal and the guild will be upgraded by 1 level, which means more positions. Guild Battle Power is shared according to guild position.
