Skill Team PK Tournament

This is a contest based on the feeling that nothing is more honorable than a fair fight. The competition has set the maximum for equipment and the character's Battle Power. The only thing that really matters is the skill of the fighters and their teamwork!

Rules and Restrictions:

1. Maximum value of equipment (including mounts): Super One Socket +4. If you have better items equipped, they will default to the maximum value.

2. Maximum level of equipment: Level 120. If you have items of a higher level equipped, they will default to Level 120.

3. Maximum Nobility: Earl. If your rank is higher than that, it will default to Earl.

4. The Battle Power you share from your Clan, Mentor, and Guild do not count.

5. Highest level of Sub-class (except Apothecary): Level 5. If your Sub-class level is higher than that, it will default to Level 5. There are no restrictions to the Apothecary.

6. There are no limits on Refinery effects, however Dragon Souls are nullified.

7. Only 60% of the total Inner-strength Attributes of the Jiang Hu will be counted.

It's show time! Come join in and see if your team has what it takes!
