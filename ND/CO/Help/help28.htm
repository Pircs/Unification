Peddle in the marketplace

Please kindly check if you have entered the correct price.


(1) Put items in your shop slots
* Drag the item to the Shop Window.
* Input your selling price.
* Sell many items by repeating the above two steps.

(2) Take back the items from your shop slots
Click on Remove on the right of an item, this item will return to your inventory.

(3) Attract buyers
Click on Slogan and type in the box your words to attract buys.

If you get disconnected while selling, the items will be in your inventory automatically.
