Alternative Equipment

Click the main/alternative equipment button to switch between your main equipment interface and alternative equipment interface.

Notes:

1. You can drag the main/alternative equipment button to the shortcut bar.

2. When you get rebirth, only the main equipment will revert to your new low level. The improper alternative equipment to your level will be unloaded automatically.

3. Upgrading is only available for equipment on the main equipment interface.

4. If some equipment slots on the alternative equipment interface are empty, the main equipment will be equipped instead.
