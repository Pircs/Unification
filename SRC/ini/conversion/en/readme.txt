Welcome to the world of Conquer!

Minimum system requirements:
CPU: 800 MHz Intel or Pentium processor
Display: 16MB
Memory: 128MB or above
If your computer doesn't reach the above requirements,  the screen may flicker and the game may be interrupted.

To run Conquer, installing DiredtX8.1 is compulsory.  Please make sure you have already installed DirectX8.1.

Please set your screen display to high color(16bit).

Enjoy yourself!
